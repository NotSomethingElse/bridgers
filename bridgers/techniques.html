Bridge Techniques

There are a variety of techniques used to win in The Bridge. Here are just a few.

Video of explained techniques

Towering

This technique is building a tower in front of your opponent to delay them, when you can get over and to the goal

Pros:

  • Easy to do
  • Hard for the enemy to counter
  • If practiced, can be executed in a few seconds

Cons:

  • Frowned upon in the community
  • Can be countered well if opponents are good

Wallrunning

This is a fundamental technique in the Bridge, jumping around obstacles and placing blocks underneath you to stay

Pros:

  • If practiced, can get around pretty much any obstacle
  • Easy to do repeatedly

Cons:

  • Easy to counter if you're close to the opponent

Bow Combo

This is the technique of hitting your enemy with an arrow to set up their knockback, which makes it easy to hit them many times after.

Pros:

  • Extremely useful in PvP
  • Basically impossible for opponent to counter

Cons:

  • Arrow might not be loaded as it has a 3 second cooldown

Building a Foundation

A good foundation is key to winning the game. setting up towers and staircases against your opponent the first match sets you up for the rest of the game.

Pros:

  • Basically guarantees the win
  • Hard to counter

Cons:

  • Takes time from your first point
